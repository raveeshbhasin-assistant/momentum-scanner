How this scanner thinks

This is a prototype scanner designed to catch the group-chat class of winners that our v3.4.2 momentum scanner filters out by design — low-float runners, theme-rotation plays, and second-order beneficiaries of peer news. It does not replace the main scanner. It runs in parallel on a disjoint universe.
