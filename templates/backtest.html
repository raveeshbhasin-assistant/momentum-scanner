Backtest idle

Replay the v3.4.2 algorithm against historical 5-minute bars. Technicals-only entries, MAE-aware exits (see logic). Backtest Filters logic is unchanged from v3.4.1; leader display mode and weak-signal floor are live-scanner-only concerns.

Read before trusting numbers. This backtest has known limitations:
  • Technicals only. News sentiment and pre-market catalyst boosts are assumed zero — headlines can't be faithfully replayed. Live composite scores will be higher on average than backtest scores.
  • No survivorship adjustment. Universe is today's list; delistings and ticker changes are not modeled.
  • Flat 0.02% slippage per side and no commissions. Wide-spread mid-caps treated the same as AAPL.
  • VIX regime uses daily close, not intraday — misses intraday VIX pops.
  • yfinance 5-min data limit is ~59 days, so start dates older than that are clamped.
  • Earnings hard-filter uses the current cache — dates earlier than current coverage effectively have no earnings context.
